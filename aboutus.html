Birthday Reminder

There are few dates as special as one's birthday, so its important that you remember to celebrate when the day comes for your friends and family members. Many websites that offer reminders, such as Facebook, infringe on your privacy and sell your data. Our website allows you to create profiles for important people in your life, and create reminders so that you never forget their birthday, without stealing your data.
